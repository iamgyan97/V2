About Nepashya Foundation

Nepashya Foundation is a registered Section 8 non-profit organization in India, guided by a simple but powerful philosophy inspired by Buddha: "Appo Deepo Bhava" — Be your own light. We believe true transformation begins within. By nurturing awareness, compassion, and knowledge, individuals can uplift themselves and society as a whole.

Our Vision

To build an empowered, aware, and compassionate society where every individual has access to quality education, opportunities for growth, and the wisdom to contribute positively to the world.

Our Mission

To enable holistic human development by providing education, life skills, and awareness, while fostering ethical values, technological understanding, and strong community relationships — especially among children, youth, and underserved communities.

Our Motive

To create a socially responsible and self-reliant generation by combining education, ethical values, and modern skills, ensuring individuals contribute meaningfully to society.

Our Core Values

Compassion (Karuna), Wisdom (Prajna), Integrity, Inclusiveness, and Self-Reliance guide everything we do.
