Our Focus Areas

Nepashya Foundation works across eight core focus areas, each designed to build a more aware, skilled, and compassionate society.

1. Education for All

Promoting accessible, inclusive, and quality education for every child.

2. Child Development & Protection

Supporting the physical, mental, and emotional growth of children.

3. Skill Development & Livelihood

Providing vocational and entrepreneurial skills for self-reliance.

4. Technology & AI Awareness

Promoting responsible and informed use of emerging technologies.

5. Community–Police Relationship Building

Strengthening trust between communities and law enforcement through dialogue.

6. Civic Responsibility & Social Harmony

Encouraging ethical, responsible, and harmonious citizenship.

7. Research, Awareness & Policy Advocacy

Conducting studies and spreading awareness on key social issues.

8. Collaboration & Partnerships

Working with stakeholders, schools, and organizations for greater impact.
